About Me

My Story

"I am a UI designer based in Denver, Colorado with a background in Apparel Design and Production. My background in Apparel Design has allowed me to develop the ability to creatively solve problems on tight deadlines, while still being able to output a functional and aesthetically pleasing product. Having eight years of experience as a sewist and designer pushes you to observe your everyday surroundings and your response to them. This has made the transition into UI design a lot easier, as the design ideation process has some overlap. Alongside my bachelor's in Apparel Design, I have completed a UI Design certification with CareerFoundry."
